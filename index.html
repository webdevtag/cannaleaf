Diesel: The Workhorse Of Our Global Economy

Diesel packs more energy than any other carbon-based fuel. It moves more goods for less, so fleet operators make more money. That’s why 90% of all global goods are transported by diesel.
However, diesel accounts for 35% of global greenhouse gas emissions, putting fleet operators under pressure to become cleaner and to transition to zero-emission fuel.

About CL Hydrolyzer The Practical Pathway To Cleaner, More Efficient Diesel

Diesel-Hydrogen Assist Technology (CL Hydrolyzer) is a certified and warrantied aftermarket onboard hydrogen generator. Through electrolysis, it separates steam distilled water into hydrogen and oxygen, producing pure hydrogen on demand. The hydrogen is infused into the fuel-air mixture of any large diesel engine to enhance the fuel burn.

By delivering hydrogen into an engine, CL Hydrolyzer increases combustion efficiency from approximately 70% up to 93%. This produces more power, burns less fuel, decreases maintenance costs, and reduces emissions.
