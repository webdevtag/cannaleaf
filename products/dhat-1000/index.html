CL Hydrolyzer 1000

The CL Hydrolyzer 1000 from Cannaleaf Hydrolyzer Technologies is our most compact hydrogen generation unit designed for semi tractors, crane operators, and other medium-to-heavy duty diesel-powered equipment. Ideal for machines with engines between 9 and 15 liters.

CL Hydrolyzer 1000 How It Works

The CL Hydrolyzer 1000 generates the correct amount of hydrogen, on-demand, to join the air/fuel mixture as it enters the cylinder for combustion.  The addition of hydrogen allows for a more complete fuel burn, almost eliminating harmful emissions, and improving fuel efficiency.

The CL Hydrolyzer 1000’s patented PEM cell produces hydrogen through electrolysis from just steam distilled water and power from the engine’s alternator. Because this process happens on-demand, there is no on-board storage of hydrogen, and the CL Hydrolyzer 1000 is completely safe.

CL Hydrolyzer Technology

Making a Positive Impact Reducing Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Diesel engines produce an estimated 35% of all greenhouse gases. Of that 35%, the largest global contributors are diesel semi tractors.

Our products enable diesel tractor engines to meet and exceed current Australia EPA emission requirements, while paying for itself in reduced fuel usage. Recent 3rd party testing validates HOD technologies on board H2 assist systems are proven to significantly reduce CO2, NOx, and Particulate Matter emissions.

About CL Hydrolyzer Technology

CL Hydrolyzer: a solution for Now

Battery Technology Isn't Ready

While battery powered machines will be transformative in the long-term, the technology simply isn’t ready for deployment today. There currently isn’t enough raw material supply to make a dent in the overall demand. And mining battery materials comes with it’s own host of environmental impact – carbon emissions, water depletion, deforestation, and toxic waste.

With no way to convert all diesel trucks to battery-powered in the immediate future, CL Hydrolyzer is the perfect transformative transitional technology.
