CL Hydrolyzer 3000

The CL Hydrolyzer 3000 from Cannaleaf Hydrolyzer Technologies is made for diesel power generators (gensets). It is ideal for generators with engines between 20 and 80 liters.

CL Hydrolyzer 3000 How It Works

The CL Hydrolyzer 3000 generates the correct amount of hydrogen, on-demand, to join the air/fuel mixture as it enters the genset engine for combustion.Â  The addition of hydrogen allows for a more complete fuel burn, almost eliminating harmful emissions, and improving fuel efficiency.

The CL Hydrolyzer 3000’s patented PEM cell produces hydrogen through electrolysis from just steam distilled water and power from the engine’s alternator. Because this process happens on-demand, there is no storage of hydrogen, and the C-L Hydrolyzer 3000 is completely safe.
