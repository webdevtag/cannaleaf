CL Hydrolyzer Products Solutions for Any Diesel Engine

Our aftermarket Diesel-Hydrogen Assist Technology (CL Hydrolyzer) onboard hydrogen generators are designed to works on all diesel engines 9 liters and larger. Our products can be outfitted on trucks, buses, gensets, trains, mining equipment, marine vessels, and most types of heavy equipment diesel engines.

Under the Hood of Diesel-Hydrogen Assist Technology (CL Hydrolyzer)

The CL Hydrolyzer 1000 generator is a ruggedized and compact device that consists of recycled and recyclable materials and contains no hazardous materials.

The top half of the CL Hydrolyzer system houses our proprietary WET fuel cell system and a water reservoir containing enough steam distilled water for up to 20,000 km of road usage (or approximately 50 operating hours).
